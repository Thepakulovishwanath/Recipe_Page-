South Indian Spiced Chicken

A flavorful and spicy chicken dish, bursting with traditional South Indian spices. Perfect for your next dinner!

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (bone-in or boneless, cut into pieces)
  • 2 tablespoons oil (coconut or sunflower)
  • 1 large onion, finely sliced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh curry leaves and coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the sliced onions and curry leaves, and saut√© until the onions are golden brown.
  2. Add the ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Stir in the chopped tomato and cook until it softens and oil starts to separate.
  4. Add the turmeric, chili, coriander, and cumin powders. Mix well and cook for a minute.
  5. Add the chicken pieces, salt, and mix to coat the chicken with spices.
  6. Cover the pan and cook on medium-low heat for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally until the chicken is tender.
  7. Sprinkle garam masala and cook for another 2-3 minutes. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
